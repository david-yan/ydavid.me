About

Hello there! I created this website in my spare time as a landing page for myself, friends, families, and anyone else who might be interested. My goal for this site is to have a place where I can share about my life, thoughts, aspirations, etc. on a platform that is not social media. This is also an opportunity for me to learn about front end website development, as my forte and preference has been in anything but. I hope that this website not only turns out to be something that I am proud to show to others, but also that the development proves to be a great learning experience for me.
